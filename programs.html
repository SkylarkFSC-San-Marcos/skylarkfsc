Community Chipper Days

Our most popular program! Chipper Days provide free chipping services for Skylark residents to dispose of cut vegetation and brush. By removing fire fuel from your property, you create defensible space and reduce wildfire risk.

  • Held twice yearly (spring and fall)
  • Free for all Skylark residents
  • Chips recycled into mulch for community use
  • Convenient drop-off locations throughout the community
See Upcoming Dates

Defensible Space Workshops

Learn how to create and maintain effective defensible space around your home. Our expert-led workshops cover vegetation management, fire-resistant landscaping, and home hardening techniques.

  • Monthly workshops during fire season
  • Hands-on demonstrations
  • Free home assessments available
  • Take-home resources and checklists
Sign Up

Emergency Preparedness Training

Be ready when disaster strikes. Our emergency preparedness training helps families create emergency plans, build go-bags, and understand evacuation procedures specific to our community.

  • Family emergency planning guidance
  • Go-bag assembly workshops
  • Evacuation route familiarization
  • Emergency communication planning
Learn More

Firewise USA Recognition

Skylark is a proud Firewise USA recognized community. This national program provides a framework for communities to organize and take action to reduce wildfire risk at the local level.

  • Annual community-wide risk assessment
  • Coordinated neighborhood action plans
  • Recognition as a fire-adapted community
  • Access to additional resources and grants
Visit Firewise USA

Youth Fire Safety Education

Teaching the next generation about fire safety. Our youth programs bring age-appropriate fire safety education to local schools and community groups.

  • School presentations and assemblies
  • Scout troop badge programs
  • Summer camp fire safety sessions
  • Interactive learning materials
