Volunteer With Us

Join our team of dedicated volunteers making a difference in the Skylark community.

Chipper Day Volunteers

Help organize and run our community chipper days. Roles include traffic direction, registration, and community outreach.

Home Assessment Team

Trained volunteers who conduct defensible space assessments for community members. Training provided.

Community Outreach

Help spread the word about fire safety through events, social media, and neighborhood outreach.

Interested in volunteering? Contact us using the form above and select "Volunteer Opportunities" as the subject.

Join Our Newsletter

Stay informed about fire safety news, upcoming events, and community updates. We send out a monthly newsletter with important information to help you stay prepared.

Our newsletter includes:

  • Upcoming event announcements
  • Seasonal fire safety tips
  • Community news and updates
  • Volunteer opportunities
  • Grant and funding updates
