Your Impact

See how your donation helps protect our community.

$25

Provides educational materials for one family to prepare for wildfire season.

$50

Funds a home defensible space assessment for a community member.

$100

Sponsors a youth fire safety education presentation at a local school.

$500

Helps fund a community chipper day, processing vegetation for dozens of homes.
